A White Heron

Plot: * Hunter offers Sylvia $10 (lot of money at the time) if anyone can find the heron * Sylvia climbs up the tallest tree to find the heron, which she does * Sylvia does not divulge the secret of the heron in the end Big Ideas: * Innocence and Nature vs Corruption of Man: Instead of giving into the temptation of money and greed presented by the hunter, Sylvia maintains her innocence and her respect toward nature by preserving the secret of the white heron.
